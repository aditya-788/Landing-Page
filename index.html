TRAVEL

THE WORLD WITH ADITYA

Travel is the movement of people between distant geographical
locations. Travel can be done by foot, bicycle, automobile, train,
bus, boat, airways or by other means, with or without luggage
and can be one way or round trip. Travel can also include relati-
vely short stays between succcessive movements, as in the case of
tourism.
